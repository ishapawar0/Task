OUR VISION

               e-Entrepreneurship Cell, RGPV is a non-profit organization that believes entrepreneurship is pivotal for
                 our country's development. It was established with the purpose of creating, fostering, and promoting 
                  the spirit of developing a culture of entrepreneurship in our surroundings.The team here works to 
                   encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their venture and dispel the fear of starting
                    a startup. We guide young entrepreneurs and technopreneurs in their journey from campus to 
                     incubators and support them in transforming their budding ideas into successful startups.

    • INITIATIVES

                To fulfill our vision to build an ecosystem where networking and entrepreneurial mindset are encouraged, we have 
            conceptualized and are successfully running the following initiatives to help future entrepreneurs in their entrepreneurial 
                                                                           journey.

    Rural
    Entrepreneurship

    An initiative where we promote the potential and creative entrepreneurial ideas from various sections of society and present them to the vast network of entrepreneurs and investors, spotlighting the unique talents.

    Startup Incubation

    An initiative by E-cell RGPV that caters to the growing startups of our country. Sharing of entrepreneurship endeavors and experiences by experts provides profuse guidance to future entrepreneurs and technopreneurs.
